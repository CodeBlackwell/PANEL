# Judicial Review


## Judge Business

Overall Score: 6.8

The project addresses a real issue in enhancing trading model accuracy using market feedback, which can appeal to financial institutions. The approach's value is clear in terms of improving trading models through more sophisticated learning techniques. However, the business model and target market need further clarification for revenue potential. The proposal is technically comprehensive but may face competition from simpler existing solutions, and timelines could be optimistic considering the complexity and potential regulatory compliance hurdles.


## Judge Technical

## Judge Feasibility

Overall Score: 7.0

The resource estimates are generally realistic, though using consumer-grade GPUs may limit scalability. External dependencies such as historical data availability seem manageable but could pose challenges. The complexity is moderately high and aligned with the proposed team’s capabilities and timeline, but the success largely depends on effective integration of multiple components.
